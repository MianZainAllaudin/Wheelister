About Wheelister

Revolutionizing car rentals in Pakistan with convenience, quality, and exceptional service.

Our Story

Our Mission

At Wheelister, our mission is to revolutionize transportation in Pakistan by providing accessible, affordable, and reliable car rental services. We strive to make every journey memorable and hassle-free.

Our Vision

We envision a future where renting a car is as easy as a few clicks. Our goal is to become Pakistan's most trusted mobility platform, connecting people with vehicles that suit their needs and lifestyle.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, and customer satisfaction guide everything we do. We believe in transparency, reliability, and building long-lasting relationships with our customers and partners.

Our Journey

  • 2023

    Founded in Islamabad with a small fleet of 10 vehicles, Wheelister set out to change how people rent cars in Pakistan.

  • 2024

    Expanded operations to Lahore and Karachi, tripling our fleet size and introducing premium luxury vehicles.

  • 2025

    Launched our website, making car rentals even more accessible. Welcomed our 5,000th customer.

  • 2026

    IA will be recognized as "Best Car Rental Service" at the Pakistan Transportation Awards. Will Expand up to 10 more cities.

  • 2026

    Today, we're serving over 10,000 customers with a fleet of 500+ vehicles across 50+ cities in Pakistan.
